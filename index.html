shell

Developed and launched a platform for Shell to collaborate with the AI energy innovators and solve energy-related challenges. An open innovation studio was launched in September 2020, offering software tools, on-demand work, prize-winning challenges, and mentorship opportunities. Over 100 community members, including O&G executives, management consulting leadership, startup CEOs, and geoscientists, were recruited and onboarded as FTE operators.

under armour

Created the web3 strategy for Under Armour over five sprints with a cross-functional team of top executives. The team arrived at a unified point of view on how the brand should enter the metaverse and established a product direction using VR for athlete training with NFT rewards. Prototypes were built to gather feedback from athletes, and a comprehensive ecosystem strategy with a roadmap for two years was developed, including a Center of Excellence.

redro

An AI-powered stylist that helps people stay fashionable by using algorithms to identify individual preferences and suggest tailored clothing options. It eliminates the guesswork from fashion choices and is available 24/7. With its advanced artificial intelligence technology, it has revolutionized the way people choose their clothes. The platform is incredibly user-friendly, and users can easily receive style recommendations.
